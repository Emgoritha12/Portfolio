Anna

I remember sitting with my old roomate Anna in the cafeteria. She and her boyfriend were messing around, and she did some thing with her hat and glasses. I thought that it was funny, so I took a picture of the look she gave. I later turned that picture into a procreate painting. I did this by tracing and painting based on the color and tint. This led me to end up with a painted semi-realistic look. This digital painting reminds me of the times I spent with my friend.
