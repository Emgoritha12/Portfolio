Off-bran Bob Ross

I decided to watch Bob Ross several years back. My dad was in his room, called me in, and introduced me to the artist and his happy trees. I actually enjoyed the show and based this painting off of one of his old paintings. The painting is not very high quality since it is one of my first paintings, but it is still something I look back at and smile. The purpose of this painting was for me to learn how to paint better through Ross's tutorial videos. It was a lot of fun painting this piece.
