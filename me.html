Self-Portrait

When I was drawing on my ipad. I decided to do another self-portrait through the app Procreate. This app provided me the opportunity to create a comic book look to me. I decided to go this route out of chance rather than really any planning. I wanted to paint myself, and see what it would look like. The result was a comic-like self-portrait of myself. I think that this piece has little to no meaning other than me just testing my artistic capabilities.
