Firefly

For this design, I decided to go off of my previous artwork. I decided to make a digital artwork example of one of my paintings I have done in the past. I have often used procreate, and because of this, I wanted to see how I could transform a physical painting into a digital painting. For me, this piece is an example of digitally transforming artwork or any physical product into a digital product. I remember spending a lot of time on the original painting, and how I watched a video to do so. I wanted to try something new and test my boundaries in art, which is why I chose to go this route. The picture represents how one piece of art can come in many forms.
