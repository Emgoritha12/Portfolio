Bipolar Disorder

This painting is what I call Bipolar Disorder. I came up with this idea several years ago. The idea came out of nowhere but the inspiration was there. Because of the rise in inspiration, I was able to produce this work. Every color means a different emotion. Red means anger, blue means depression, green means envy, yellow means euphoria, and purple is a mix between mania and depression. Purple is the in between where you don't know which emotion you have, but you still feel all of them. To me, purple is the worst emotion to have because it is the most painful.
