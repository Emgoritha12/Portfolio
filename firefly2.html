Firefly (Original)

I remember, during 2020, my ladies church group help a zoom painting meeting. The goal was to follow along to different paintings. Everyone chose some kind of tree one, but I chose to do the fireflies in a jar. For some reason, it had become my favorite painting I had ever made. It was a simple design, yet very bright. I later turned this painting into a digital art that was much more vibrant. The purpose of this painting was to create something alike, yet also unique.
